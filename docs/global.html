Unmutes the audio by setting the `muted` property of the `AUDIO` and `LOST_AUDIO` variables to `false`. Finally, it updates the `src` attribute of the `speakerIcon` element to "./img/Icons/mute.png" and calls the `unmute()` method of the `world` object.
